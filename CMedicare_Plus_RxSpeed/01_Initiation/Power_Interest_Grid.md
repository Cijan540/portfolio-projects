## Engagement Rationale
Effort is concentrated on the sponsor and CPO, who hold both decision authority
and high stake in the outcome. IT, Operations, and Compliance hold blocking power
and are kept satisfied through phase-gate involvement. The large base of end users
is kept informed and trained, as their adoption ultimately determines success.
