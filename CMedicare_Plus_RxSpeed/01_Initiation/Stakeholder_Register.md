## Key Stakeholder Notes

**Ishan Rai (Sponsor)** — The most critical stakeholder. Holds budget authority
and project sponsorship. All major decisions and escalations route through him.
Success is measured against the objectives he approved in the Charter.

**Dr. Harshada Bisht (CPO)** — Guards clinical quality and patient safety. Any
solution must not compromise dispensing accuracy or compliance. Must be consulted
before changes that touch the clinical workflow.

**Samrat Karki (IT)** — Controls data access and infrastructure. Critical enabler;
without IT cooperation, the dashboard cannot be built or deployed. High influence
despite medium interest.

**Tina Mims (Compliance)** — Has effective veto power on anything touching PHI.
HIPAA compliance is non-negotiable, so compliance review is built into every phase.

**Store Managers (247)** — The primary users. High interest (it affects their daily
work) but low individual influence. Their adoption determines whether the project
succeeds in practice, making change management and training essential.
