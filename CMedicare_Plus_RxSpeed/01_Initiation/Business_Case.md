## 1. Executive Summary

CMedicare Plus is losing an estimated **$88M annually** due to deteriorating
prescription fulfillment performance. Over 24 months, average wait times have
risen 59% (from 19.2 to 30.6 minutes), driving customer dissatisfaction and
attrition. This business case proposes a **$485,000 investment** in an
analytics-driven solution projected to recover **$35M annually** — a payback
period of under one week of recovered revenue and a first-year ROI exceeding
7,000%.

## 3. The Proposed Solution

A centralized analytics platform (Tableau dashboard) that:
- Provides real-time visibility into wait times and bottlenecks
- Offers role-based views for executives, district managers, store managers, and pharmacists
- Enables data-driven staffing decisions during peak windows
- Surfaces underperforming stores and regions for targeted intervention
