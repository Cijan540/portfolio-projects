## 2. Project Background

CMedicare Plus Company, a 247-store retail pharmacy chain operating across 12
US states, has experienced a significant decline in prescription fulfillment
performance over the past 24 months. Analysis of operational data reveals that
average wait times have increased substantially, driving down customer
satisfaction and putting revenue at risk. The executive team has authorized
this initiative to design and deliver an analytics-driven solution that enables
pharmacy leadership and store managers to identify bottlenecks and improve
fulfillment efficiency.

---

## 3. Business Problem Statement

A 24-month baseline analysis of operational data identified the following:

- **Average prescription wait time increased from 19.2 to 30.6 minutes**
  (a 59% increase), now double the 15-minute industry target
- **Approximately 70% of prescriptions exceed the 15-minute target**
- **Customer satisfaction collapses with wait time** — from 9.5 (under 10 min)
  to 3.4 (over 30 min)
- **A systematic regional gap exists** — Midwest stores average 24.8 min vs
  19.0 min in the Southeast (~30% worse)
- **Estimated $88M in annual revenue is at risk** through customer attrition (4.2%)

Operational data is currently fragmented across multiple systems, preventing
managers from diagnosing root causes or taking timely action.

## 4. Project Objectives (SMART)

| # | Objective |
|---|-----------|
| 1 | Reduce average prescription wait time from 30.6 to 15 minutes within 6 months of deployment |

| 4 | Recover an estimated $35M in annual revenue within 12 months |
| 5 | Reduce the Midwest-Southeast performance gap from 5.8 min to under 2 min |

## 5. Project Scope

### In Scope
- Analysis of the prescription fulfillment process from intake to pickup
- Integration of operational, satisfaction, and workforce data
- Development of a Tableau dashboard with role-based views (Executive, District, Store, Pharmacist)
- Pilot rollout to 10 stores, followed by phased deployment to all 247
- User training and documentation
- HIPAA-compliant data handling

### Out of Scope
- Modifications to the core Pharmacy Management System
- Mobile app changes
- Insurance/payer system integration
- Inventory ordering and supplier management
- Changes to clinical or dispensing protocols
