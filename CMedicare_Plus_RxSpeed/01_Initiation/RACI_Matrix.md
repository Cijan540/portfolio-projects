## Key Observations

- **The BA is Responsible for most analytical and documentation work** — 
  this reflects the central coordinating role of the business analyst.
- **The Sponsor (Ishan Rai) is Accountable for major milestones** — he owns the 
  business outcome and holds sign-off authority.
- **IT (Samrat Karki) is Accountable for technical deliverables** — the data 
  dictionary and dashboard development sit with the technology team.
- **Compliance (Tina Mims) is Accountable for the HIPAA review** — a deliberate 
  separation of duties to ensure regulatory rigor.
- **Every activity has exactly one Accountable party** — preventing ownership gaps.
